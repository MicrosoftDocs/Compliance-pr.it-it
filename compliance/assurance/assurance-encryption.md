## <a name="how-does-microsoft-365-manage-the-keys-used-for-encryption"></a><span data-ttu-id="661ab-122">In che modo Microsoft 365 gestisce le chiavi utilizzate per la crittografia?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="661ab-122">How does Microsoft 365 manage the keys used for encryption?</span></span>
